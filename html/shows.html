Faithful TV Shows

This is a list of faithful TV shows that I feel are inspired by the Holy Spirit, some more so than others. Whenever I watch these shows I see God's glory in my life and it strengthens my faith. When I watch these shows I see God's abundance, because I can always find something from this list to lift my soul. Jesus spoke of God's abundance in Matthew 10:23 when he said, "If people in one city persecute you, simply flee to another one, because I honestly say to you, that you will not go through all the cities of Israel before the Son of Man returns." While not all of these shows were produced as a statement of faith, some were and this demonstrates to me that God uses all types of people for his purposes. While many of them are supported by commercials, I definitely feel that God played a role in their production.



Biblical Collector's Series

Documentary · 2006 · Run Time 1 hr each · TV-PG
Starring: Chuck Missler, David W. Balsiger, Roger Oakland
Directed by: David Priest

Are Bible stories an accurate historical record of events or the musings of mortal men? Biblical mysteries are examined in this series that asks, "What is the real truth?" A far reaching investigation of questions asked by scholars, scientists and theologians through the ages about the Bible from Adam & Eve to the early years of Jesus and what archeologists, paleontologists, biologists and theologians are saying today.

Highway to Heaven

Drama · 1989 · about 1 hr each · TV-PG
Starring: Michael Landon, Victor French, James Troesh, Margie Impert, H. Richard Greene
Directed by: Michael Landon, Victor French, Dan Gordon, William F. Claxton

In the series opener, we meet Jonathan Smith, a probationary angel who has been sent to help people on Earth. He befriends residents at a retirement home. The administrator at the home has a brother named Mark who is suspicious of Jonathan.

More About - Highway to Heaven


Jesus of Nazareth Complete Story

Drama · 1977 · 6 hr 21 min · TV-PG
Starring: Robert Powell, Anne Bancroft, Enrest Borgnine, James Farentino, Ian McShane, Christopher Plummer
Directed by: Franco Zeffirelli

Acclaimed director Franco Zeffirelli and an all-star cast come together to deliver a powerful adaptation of the Gospels in 1977's Jesus Of Nazareth. From the Nativity to the Crucifixion and Resurrection, the life of Jesus is presented with stunning depth, gravity, and emotion. After Joseph and Mary's betrothal, Mary is visited by the angel Gabriel, who tells her she will bear the Son of God. Mary and Joseph travel to Bethlehem to take part in Herod's census, where Jesus is born. - Jesus meets Mary Magdalene and John the Baptist and continues to perform miracles among his people. - Judas implores Zerah to let Jesus appear before the Sanhedrin in Jerusalem, where "He will be handed over to the unbelievers. - Christ is condemned, crucified, dies on the cross, and is resurrected on the third day.

More About - Jesus of Nazareth


The Chosen

Drama · TV Series · 2017-2024 · NR
Starring: Elizabeth Tabish, Jonathan Roumie, Noah James, Paras Patel, Shahar Isaac
Directed by: Dallas Jenkins

The Chosen is an American Christian historical drama television series. It is the first multi-season series about the life and ministry of Jesus of Nazareth pt. 1. Primarily set in Judaea and Galilee in the 1st century, the series centers on Jesus and the people who met and followed him.

The Twelve Apostles: The First 12 Men Chosen by Jesus

Documentary · TV Series · 2003 · TV-G
Starring: Melvyn Bragg, Mark Goodacre
Directed by: Bob Bee

Why did Jesus select the men he did to be his disciples, followers, and companions? This series examines the well-known and obscure men among them that he appointed to be his apostles.
